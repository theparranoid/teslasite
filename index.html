What is laser maze?

Laser maze is a futuristic attraction. Player is challenged to get through the room filled with lots of laser beams without touching them. Just like in spy movies.

If player touches a beam, he triggers an light and sound alarm. At the end of the game player can look at his stat sheet on the screen.

Tesla lasers offers 4 different game modes: spy (single player), team, survival, destroyer.
